## The Stack

I chose **Jekyll** because GitHub Pages builds it automatically — no CI
pipeline or build step on my end. Pushing markdown to `main` is enough.

For the theme, **Minimal Mistakes** hit the right balance of
customizability and out-of-the-box functionality. The dark skin was a
requirement.
